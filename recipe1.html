chicken manchrian

Ingredients

  • **For the Chicken Balls**

  • 250 grams boneless chicken, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • Oil for deep frying

  • **For manchurian sauce**

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions,capsicum
  • 2 tablespoons tomato ketchup,soy sauce
  • 1 tablesppon of vinegar,red chilly sauce
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

      for chicken balls----

  1. In a bowl, combine finely chopped chicken, egg, all-purpose flour, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and black pepper powder.
  2. Mix the ingredients well to form a thick batter.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
  4. Drop small portions of the chicken batter into the hot oil and fry until they turn golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Remove the chicken balls and place them on a paper towel to absorb excess oil. Set aside.
